PRIVACY POLICY FOR COINSIM-PRO
Last Updated: March 31, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
This Privacy Policy explains how CoinSim-Pro ("we," "our," or "the App") handles information. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency regarding data practices.

2. DATA COLLECTION AND USAGE
CoinSim-Pro does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

No Personal Account: You can use the App without creating an account or providing personal details.

Simulation Data: All trading simulation data (virtual balances, history) is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES (ADVERTISING AND ANALYTICS)
To provide and monetize our service, we use third-party tools that may collect technical data:

Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. Google may use your device's advertising ID (GAID/IDFA) to show personalized or non-personalized ads.

User Messaging Platform (UMP): We use Google's UMP to collect consent for personalized advertising from users in certain regions (e.g., EEA/UK).

Google Play Services: The App utilizes Google Play Services for core functionalities.

4. COOKIES AND IDENTIFIERS
Third-party advertising partners (Google AdMob) may use cookies or unique device identifiers to serve ads based on your interests and app usage. You can manage these preferences through your device settings or the "Manage Privacy Options" link within the App.

5. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
CoinSim-Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can take necessary action.

6. SECURITY
We take reasonable measures to protect the App's integrity. However, since the App does not collect personal data, the risk to your personal privacy is minimized.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

8. CONTACT US
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
